About Me

I work to produce insights from data for business partners, customers, or anyone who wants to make decisions from data. Experienced in healthcare analytics across a broad spectrum of patient care perspectives.


I program primarily in R and Python, using statistical/data packages to clean, manipulate, and analyze data. I'm also certified in SAS. I am proficient in SQL, writing queries to update and search across databases with millions of records. In addition to my programming proficiencies, I use PowerBI and the Microsoft analytics suite (Power Query, DAX, etc.) to provide a unified, consistent, and secure analytics output to end-users.

I'm currently a data analyst at Patient Funding Alternatives, and am pursuing my Master's of Science in Applied Data Analytics at Boston University.
